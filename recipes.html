Pro Tips

Getting the Most
From Your Bottarga

Use bottarga like a fine cheese — at room temperature, sliced thin with a sharp non-serrated knife. Or grate it like a truffle over anything hot. The heat opens up the flavor.

✦

Always bring bottarga to room temperature before serving

✦

Grate directly over hot food — the heat blooms the flavor

✦

Less is more when you first start — the flavor is intense

✦

Pair with Arak, dry white wine, or prosecco as an aperitif

✦

Re-wrap tightly after opening; store refrigerated at all times
