Common Questions

FAQ

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship to Canada and internationally. US orders come with free USPS shipping. Contact us for Canada and international shipping rates.

How should I store bottarga after it arrives?

Always keep bottarga refrigerated. If the original seal is intact, it will keep for several months. Once opened, wrap tightly in plastic film removing all air pockets — shrink wrap is ideal. Consume within a few weeks of opening.

What's the difference between waxed and shrink-wrapped bottarga?

Both are equally authentic preservation methods — wax is the older, traditional method; shrink wrap is modern. The wax simply needs to be peeled off before serving (it comes off easily at room temperature). The bottarga inside is identical.

Which bottarga do you recommend for first-timers?

We suggest starting with Ouro do Brasil — it has the mildest, sweetest flavor and is the most approachable for those new to bottarga. From there, graduate to Sardinian Gold or Boutargue Classique for more complex, traditional profiles.
